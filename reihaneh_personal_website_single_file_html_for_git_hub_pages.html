About

I’m Reihaneh Arjmandi — a philosopher and psychologist interested in how mind, body, and world intertwine. I hold an MSc (Conversion) in Philosophy from the University of Glasgow (Merit) and a BSc in Psychology from Shahid Beheshti University (Distinction). My research explores embodied and 4E cognition, phenomenology, perception, and the philosophy of mind.

Based in the UK • Second home: Glasgow
